# Example 04 — Audio Narrative: The Missed Bus

**Type:** Audio narrative  
**CEFR Level:** A2/B1  
**Target language:** English (learner L1: Japanese)

---

| Dimension | Level | Confidence |
|-----------|-------|------------|

## Audio scenario

**Duration:** 35 seconds (A2-appropriate per the 15-40s limit)  
**Speaker:** Female, neutral accent  
**Content:** Short story about missing the bus

### Transcript

"I had a terrible morning yesterday. I woke up late because my alarm didn't go off. I rushed to get ready — just five minutes in the shower, no breakfast, no coffee. I ran to the bus stop, but when I got there, I saw my bus driving away. I was so frustrated! I had to wait 25 minutes for the next bus. I arrived at work 40 minutes late. My boss wasn't happy. Next time, I'll set two alarms."

### Comprehension questions (gist)

**System:** What is the main problem in this story?

**Learner:** She missed the bus.

**System:** Correct. Why did she miss it?

**Learner:** Because she woke up late.

### Comprehension questions (detail)

**System:** How long did she wait for the next bus?

**Learner:** Twenty-five minutes.

**System:** How late was she to work?

**Learner:** Forty minutes.

### Comprehension questions (inference)

**System:** How did she feel when she saw the bus driving away?

**Learner:** She was... frustrated.

**System:** Yes! And what will she do differently next time?

**Learner:** Set two alarms.

## Retelling task

**System:** Now, tell the story back to me in your own words. I'll give you some key words: yesterday, woke up late, rushed, ran, bus, arrived late.

**Learner:** Yesterday she had a terrible morning. She woke up late because alarm didn't work. She rushed but she missed the bus. She was very frustrated. She waited 25 minutes. She arrived work 40 minutes late. Boss not happy.

**System:** Almost perfect! Two small things: "the alarm didn't go off" and "she arrived **at** work." Otherwise, very good retelling — you included all the key points.

## Personal connection

**System:** Has something like this ever happened to you?

**Learner:** Yes, last month I missed my train. I also woke up late. I was very stressed.

**System:** What did you do?

**Learner:** I took the next train. I called my boss. It was OK.
