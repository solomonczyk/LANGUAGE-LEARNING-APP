## Visual scenario

A sequence of 4 images depicting:
1. Person wakes up, looks at phone — shocked expression (overslept)
2. Person rushes to bathroom, toothbrush in mouth
3. Person runs to bus stop, bus leaving
4. Person arrives at work, boss looking at clock

## Step 1: Description

**System:** Look at picture 1. What do you see?

**Learner:** A man. He is in bed. He look at phone. He is... surprised.

**System:** Good! Why is he surprised?

**Learner:** Because... the time. It is late.

## Step 2: Sequencing

**System:** Now look at all 4 pictures. What happens first, second, third, fourth?

**Learner:** First, he wake up late. Second, he go to bathroom. Third, he run to bus but bus go. Four, he arrive work late.

**System:** Good sequence! Let's work on the verbs. Instead of "he wake up" → "he **wakes** up" (he + wakes). Try again.

**Learner:** First, he wakes up late.

## Step 3: Full narrative

**System:** Now tell the whole story in order. I'll help with time words: First, Then, After that, Finally.

**Learner:** First, he wakes up late. Then he goes to bathroom. After that, he runs to bus stop but the bus leaves. Finally, he arrives at work late.

**System:** Excellent! That's a clear, complete story!

## Transfer task

**System:** Now think about YOUR morning yesterday. Was it normal or was something different?

**Learner:** Yesterday I wake up early. I take shower, I eat breakfast. I go to work on time.

**System:** Almost there with the past tense. Let's focus on this in our next lesson!
