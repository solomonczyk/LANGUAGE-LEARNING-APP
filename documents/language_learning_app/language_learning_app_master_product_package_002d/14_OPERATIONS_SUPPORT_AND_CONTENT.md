# Operations, Support, and Content Model

**Status:** CANONICAL  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-06-10  
**Owner:** Product Owner  
**Change control:** Changes require a documented change request or ADR where specified.

## Support model by stage

- Internal alpha: direct operator support.
- Learner alpha: one support channel, response target within one business day.
- Closed beta: structured FAQ, issue tagging, escalation.
- Paid pilot: documented support hours, refund/escalation workflow.

## Support categories

- access/auth;
- lesson state;
- content quality;
- AI feedback concern;
- payment;
- privacy/deletion;
- safety;
- bug;
- feature request.

## Escalation

Critical safety/security: immediate HOLD of affected flow.  
Payment/privacy: Product Owner plus legal review boundary.  
Learning-quality dispute: methodology reviewer sample.

## Content operations

Each canonical lesson template requires:

- owner;
- version;
- source/provenance;
- learning goal;
- difficulty;
- validation status;
- reviewer;
- retirement policy.

## Human review

Before public MVP, sample review must cover:

- linguistic correctness;
- pedagogical appropriateness;
- cultural sensitivity;
- ambiguity;
- unsafe or misleading feedback;
- learner voice preservation.

## Incident response

Every production-planned incident requires:

- detection;
- containment;
- user impact;
- root cause;
- remediation;
- evidence;
- release decision.
