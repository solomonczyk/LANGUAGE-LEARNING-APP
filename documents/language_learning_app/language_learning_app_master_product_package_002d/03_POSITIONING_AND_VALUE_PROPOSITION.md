# Positioning and Value Proposition

**Status:** CANONICAL  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-06-10  
**Owner:** Product Owner  
**Change control:** Changes require a documented change request or ADR where specified.

## Positioning statement

For adults who once studied English but lost active confidence, this product is a personalized language-recovery system that teaches them to express their own thoughts through real stories and situations, unlike generic phrase drills or unrestricted AI chat.

## Category

Personalized active-language recovery and guided communication practice.

## Rational promise

The learner receives a structured sequence from diagnostic profile to independent output, delayed review, and evidence-based mastery.

## Emotional promise

“Your English is not gone. We help you reactivate it through things you genuinely want to say.”

## Proof mechanism

- multidimensional diagnostic;
- learning entry contract;
- personal narrative lesson;
- guided correction;
- independent retry;
- dialogue or transfer task;
- delayed review;
- progress evidence.

## Differentiation

1. Personal stories and real-life problems are core learning material.
2. Communicative goal is above grammar topic.
3. AI proposes; deterministic rules decide.
4. Quiz is not mastery.
5. Progress requires independent, transferred, and retained use.
6. Mixed native/target-language scaffolding is allowed.
7. The product tracks skill asymmetry rather than only a single CEFR level.

## Canonical message hierarchy

1. Restore the ability to express your own thoughts.
2. Practice real situations rather than memorize чужие фразы.
3. Receive a personal path based on what you can actually do.
4. See proof of progress through repeated independent use.

## Forbidden claims

Do not claim:

- guaranteed fluency;
- exact CEFR improvement without validated assessment;
- replacement of a qualified teacher for every learner;
- medical or psychological outcomes;
- legal compliance certification without review;
- “native speech in 30 days”;
- market-leading status without evidence.
