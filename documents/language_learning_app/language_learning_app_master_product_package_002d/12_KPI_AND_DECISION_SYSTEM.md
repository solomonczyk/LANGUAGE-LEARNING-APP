# KPI and Decision System

**Status:** CANONICAL  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-06-10  
**Owner:** Product Owner  
**Change control:** Changes require a documented change request or ADR where specified.

## North-star learning metric

Percentage of active learners who complete an independent, context-relevant output and later demonstrate delayed recall or transfer.

## Activation metrics

- onboarding completion;
- diagnostic completion;
- Learning Entry Contract viewed;
- first lesson started;
- first lesson completed;
- time to first value.

## Learning metrics

- independent output completion;
- major-error recurrence;
- guided-to-independent transition;
- transfer-task pass;
- delayed recall;
- human/AI assessment agreement;
- learner confidence delta.

## Retention metrics

- Day-1 return;
- Day-7 return;
- Day-30 return;
- lessons per active week;
- review completion;
- streak recovery, not only streak length.

## Commercial metrics

- landing conversion;
- diagnostic conversion;
- trial conversion;
- paid conversion;
- activation after payment;
- refund rate;
- average revenue per payer;
- acquisition cost;
- support cost;
- AI cost per learner.

## Quality metrics

- failed submissions;
- invalid AI outputs;
- false lesson completion;
- visual critical defects;
- crash-free sessions;
- API p95;
- audit completeness.

## Stage KPI rule

A stage may have at most:

- one primary outcome metric;
- three to five supporting metrics;
- explicit zero-tolerance metrics.

Do not optimize vanity metrics at the expense of learning or retention.
