# Budget and Resource Limits

**Status:** CANONICAL  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-06-10  
**Owner:** Product Owner  
**Change control:** Changes require a documented change request or ADR where specified.

## Budget philosophy

This is a validation-first product. Spend increases only after evidence.

## Stage budgets in EUR

| Stage | Planned | Maximum | Main spend |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| S0 Master canon | 0–100 | 150 | research/tools |
| S1 Vertical slice | 150 | 350 | hosting/dev tools/test services |
| S2 Acceptance | 50 | 150 | device/browser testing |
| S3 Real AI | 100 | 250 | provider usage/evaluation |
| S4 Internal alpha | 100 | 250 | infrastructure/monitoring |
| S5 Learner alpha | 200 | 500 | incentives/support/AI |
| S6 Closed beta | 500 | 1,200 | infrastructure/support/content |
| S7 Paid pilot | 500 | 1,500 | payment/legal/marketing/support |
| S8 Decision | 100 | 300 | analysis/legal review |

Total planned validation budget: approximately €1,700.  
Total maximum before public MVP decision: €4,650.

## Monthly infrastructure guardrail before public MVP

Maximum recurring infrastructure and SaaS: €150/month unless approved by change request.

## Marketing guardrail

Paid acquisition before proven Day-7 retention and payment: maximum €150 total.

## AI cost guardrail

- development/evaluation: €250 cumulative through S3;
- alpha: ≤€3 per active learner/month;
- paid pilot target: ≤€2 per active learner/month.

## Time budget

The Product Owner must track founder/team hours per stage. A stage exceeding 125% of its planned time requires HOLD and scope review.

## Budget breach response

No silent overspend. Required decision:

- reduce scope;
- extend only with evidence;
- change provider;
- change experiment;
- HOLD;
- STOP.
