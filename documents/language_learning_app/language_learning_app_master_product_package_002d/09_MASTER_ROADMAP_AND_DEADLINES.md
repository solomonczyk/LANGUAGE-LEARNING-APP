## Baseline calendar

The baseline assumes a small team or founder-led project. Dates are explicit to prevent endless development. Changes require the change-control process.

## Dependency rule

A stage starts only when the previous stage has an accepted proof artifact and explicit decision.

## Schedule tolerance

- up to 10% slippage: owner note;
- 10–25%: formal HOLD review;
- above 25%: scope reduction or PIVOT decision;
- no automatic deadline extension.

## Public MVP release window

Earliest controlled public MVP decision: 2027-02-03.

A public launch before this date is permitted only if all gates are met early; deadlines are not permission to bypass acceptance.
