# Go-to-Market Strategy

**Status:** CANONICAL  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-06-10  
**Owner:** Product Owner  
**Change control:** Changes require a documented change request or ADR where specified.

## Launch approach

Do not begin with broad paid advertising. Begin with a narrow founder-led, evidence-rich launch.

## Primary acquisition channels

1. Ukrainian and Russian-speaking relocation communities in Europe.
2. English-for-work and job-search communities.
3. Personal LinkedIn and professional network.
4. Telegram and Facebook groups where self-promotion is permitted.
5. Partnerships with small language tutors or career coaches.
6. Referral from pilot learners.

## Content pillars

- “I understand, but I cannot speak.”
- English after a long break.
- Before/after learner story.
- Personal narrative correction without replacing the learner’s voice.
- Visual retelling challenge.
- Audio retelling challenge.
- Everyday problem-solving English.
- Work and interview communication.
- How the diagnostic finds uneven skills.
- Why quizzes are not proof of mastery.

## Funnel

`content/outreach → landing → diagnostic preview → trial lesson → pilot offer → payment → activation → first lesson completion → week-one return → 30-day completion`

## Funnel acceptance thresholds for pilot readiness

- landing-to-diagnostic start: ≥20%;
- diagnostic completion: ≥55%;
- trial lesson start after diagnostic: ≥60%;
- trial lesson completion: ≥55%;
- offer view to paid pilot: ≥8% from qualified users;
- paid learner activation within 48 hours: ≥80%.

Thresholds may be revised only after a documented cohort analysis.

## Messaging tests

Test no more than three lead messages:

A. Restore active English after a break.  
B. Learn to express your own thoughts, not memorize чужие фразы.  
C. Practical English for relocation and work situations.

## Launch assets

Before learner alpha:

- landing page;
- diagnostic preview;
- one recorded demo;
- FAQ;
- privacy summary;
- pilot terms;
- onboarding email/message;
- support channel;
- interview script;
- consent form for research feedback.

## Paid media rule

No material paid-media budget before:

- working activation funnel;
- Day-7 retention evidence;
- paid conversion evidence;
- support workload evidence.
